Legal & Privacy Policy

Protecting your data. Respecting your rights. Building trust.

Who We Are

The African Medical Registry (AMR) is a continental health verification infrastructure operated under a legal entity headquartered in Accra, Ghana, and governed by a multi-country board of directors and health advisors. All services provided are in partnership with legally recognized national health regulatory bodies across Africa.

AMR operates in compliance with:

  • African Union data protection conventions
  • National digital health regulations
  • Licensing laws across medical, pharmacy, nursing, and traditional sectors

What Information We Collect

For Practitioners:

  • Name, gender, date of birth
  • License number and issuing authority
  • Specialization and professional type
  • National ID (secured off-chain)
  • Uploaded documents (certifications, ID)
  • Contact details (email/phone)

For Users and Visitors:

  • IP address and session data
  • Browser type and usage analytics
  • Contact information when submitting forms

All personally identifiable information (PII) is encrypted and protected under the strictest industry and regional data protection standards.

Purpose of Data Use

We use your data to:

  • Verify healthcare credentials with official regulators
  • Generate digital verification profiles and QR codes
  • Enable search and authentication across AMR platforms
  • Communicate updates and approvals to practitioners
  • Improve system integrity and prevent fraud

Data is never sold to third parties.

For practitioners, only public-safe data is displayed to users (e.g., name, license status, specialization). Sensitive details are accessible only to verified institutions through secured dashboards.

How We Protect Your Information

Our infrastructure includes:

Encrypted data at rest and in transit
Multi-factor authentication for access
Role-based permissions for different user types
Secure document vaults (IPFS or AWS GovCloud)
Blockchain-anchored license hashes (tamper-proof)

All system activity is logged and regularly audited.

International and Regional Legal Compliance

AMR complies with:

  • African Union Convention on Cybersecurity and Personal Data Protection
  • Ghana Data Protection Act, Nigeria Data Protection Law, and equivalents in each country
  • GDPR (for users accessing from the EU)
  • WHO and AU guidance on digital health identity systems

Your Consent Matters

By registering on AMR or using our verification tools, you consent to:

  • Your professional license status being publicly verified
  • Your credentials being processed and validated with national regulators
  • Secure storage of your documents for compliance purposes

You may request deletion, correction, or restriction of your data at any time by emailing: {{'privacy@africanmedicalregistry.com'}}

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Track verification usage for system monitoring
  • Enable faster loading and secure logins

You may disable cookies in your browser settings. Third-party analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) are used in compliance with privacy regulations.

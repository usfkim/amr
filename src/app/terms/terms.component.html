Welcome to the African Medical Registry

By accessing this website or using any verification, registration, or institutional tools on the African Medical Registry (AMR) platform, you agree to comply with the following Terms of Use. These terms govern your rights, responsibilities, and acceptable conduct on this platform.

Definitions

AMR

African Medical Registry, the digital platform and its affiliated country-level systems (e.g., GMR, NMR).

Practitioner

Any healthcare professional registered or applying for verification through AMR.

User

Any visitor, citizen, hospital, embassy, or institution accessing AMR to verify a practitioner.

Regulator

Official body or agency integrated into AMR for license validation and oversight.

Platform

The full digital system including website, mobile tools, dashboards, APIs, and databases.

Acceptable Use Policy

You agree to:

  • Use AMR solely for lawful and ethical purposes
  • Search for practitioners only when relevant to health, employment, immigration, or official inquiry
  • Not impersonate or misrepresent your identity
  • Not attempt to access restricted parts of the system without authorization
  • Not use bots, crawlers, or data scrapers on AMR platforms

Practitioner Responsibility

If you are a registered practitioner on AMR, you are responsible for:

  • Providing truthful, complete, and accurate information during registration
  • Updating your profile if your license status or details change
  • Respecting the confidentiality of your verification QR code or profile link
  • Reporting suspected fraud or impersonation immediately

False information or fraudulent registration may result in suspension or reporting to your national regulatory council.

Public Access Rules

  • Basic practitioner verification is open to the public
  • Public views include only non-sensitive information (e.g., name, license number, profession, validity)
  • AMR does not publish national IDs, addresses, phone numbers, or personal files without consent
  • AMR reserves the right to anonymize or redact profiles where privacy laws require

Institutional Use

For Regulators, Embassies, and Government Agencies:

By accessing the AMR institutional portal or dashboards:

  • You confirm your authority and legal mandate to view or upload practitioner data
  • You are responsible for the security of your login credentials and admin users
  • You agree to uphold AMR's data confidentiality and avoid unauthorized disclosure
  • You accept that all system actions are logged and auditable

Breach of institutional terms may lead to restricted access or legal action.

Intellectual Property

All content, software, logos, branding, databases, and interfaces within AMR are protected by copyright and intellectual property laws. You may not:

  • Copy, replicate, or sell AMR's software or registry framework
  • Modify or create derivative works of AMR materials
  • Use AMR's name or content for commercial gain without permission

Suspension & Termination

AMR reserves the right to suspend or terminate access for any user or institution found to be:

  • Misusing verification tools
  • Submitting fraudulent data
  • Abusing or hacking the system
  • Violating national or AU data protection laws

Disclaimers

AMR is a credential verification system, not a clinical certification body. It does not:

  • Guarantee practitioner competence beyond credential validation
  • Intervene in malpractice cases or clinical conduct
  • Offer legal endorsements or immigration approvals

Verification should always be supported by direct inquiry to national councils when necessary.

Jurisdiction & Dispute Resolution

AMR operates under the laws of the Republic of Ghana and is subject to pan-African policy frameworks.

In case of disputes:

  • Efforts will first be made to resolve through direct mediation
  • Failing that, matters may be escalated to arbitration or appropriate legal forums in Accra, Ghana

Updates to Terms

These terms may be updated periodically. All users are advised to review the latest version before continued use. Updates will be posted clearly on this page with the last revision date.
